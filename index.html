Story of us

Aplus Documentation and Consultancy Services CO. started on 2015 when Mrs. Noeme had the opportunity to quit her job as an accounting officer at Berns Accounting Services where she spent 5 years serving the company. She had the opportunity to partner with Kuya Nong as her first client and she managed to handle the custom's brokerage business of with the help of her accounting expertise.

As of today she founded two branches in Paranaque city and Manila city offering all sort of accounting services to help business owners focus more on their business plans.
